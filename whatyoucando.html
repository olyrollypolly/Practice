What You Can Do

Recycling does matter. Every bottle that is recycled make an impact. Plastic bottles are not biodegradable, and when they are thrown away they sit in landfills, making them take up more space and more of the enviroment
  • - Recycling is one of the best things to do, to cut back the size of landfills
  • - Using less packaged products can save space and prevent land fills from expanding
  • - To cut back on e-waste, repair phone screens and get new memory cards instead of buying a whole new appliance
  • - Composting food waste can give you great soil for your garden and cut back on food waste
